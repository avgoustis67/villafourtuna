Located 10 mi from Corfutown city/airport and 40 m from the beach, is a building complex with fully equipped apartments.

Amphitheatrically built, offers panoramic view to the sea.

Apartment Amenities:
  • Free air conditioning
  • TV & Wi-Fi
  • Private bathroom
  • Kitchenware & fridge
  • Furnished balcony with views to the sea or to the beautiful green garden

There is also free private car parking and the bus stop is just in front of the building.

At a distance of 200 – 300m there are restaurants, super market, bar, cafeterias and tourist shops.
